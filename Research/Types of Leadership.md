### Security Leadership Styles 

- **Autocratic Leader**
	**Characteristics:** *Strong Control, Clear direction, Quick Decision Making. Enforces exact directives ('do as I say')*
	
	**Advantages:** *Effective during crises, fast implementation*
	
	**Disadvantages:** *Reduced collaboration, possible employee resistance*
	
- **Democratic**
	**Characteristics:** *Collaborative, Encourages participation, Team-oriented
	
	**Advantages:** *Better Communication, increased engagement*
	
	**Disadvantages:** *Slower Decision-Making*
