
Organizations **collect information** from a vast ***array of sources – databases, customer relationship management (CRM) systems, social media platforms, apps, cloud services and APIs.*** 
This creates a crucial need for data integration and data management.

**Data Management**
	Encompasses the entire life cycle of data – from collection and storage to analysis and utilization. It ensures data accuracy, consistency, and accessibility for various business needs. Effective data management relies heavily on successful data integration.

 **Data Integration**
	 Refers to the process of combining data from disparate sources into a unified format. Imagine it as building a bridge between isolated islands of information, allowing them to connect and share their knowledge. This eliminates data silos, where valuable data remains trapped within specific systems, hindering its overall usefulness.
